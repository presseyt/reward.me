As you may be aware, it’s been in the news that usernames, passwords and other digital account information have recently been obtained as a result of security breaches from prominent sites, like Yahoo, LinkedIn and others.

To protect your personal information and your valuable More Rewards points, you will be required to change your password. This will ensure your More Rewards account remains secure, even if online accounts stored on other websites are compromised.

Please check your Spam folder in your email program if you don’t receive the password reset email within 15-20 minutes.
